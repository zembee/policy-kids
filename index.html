Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kids POS Simulator ("we," "our," or "the App") handles information. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Kids POS Simulator does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

  • We do not ask for names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal details.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking software that identifies individual users.

2. Camera Usage

The App may request access to your device's Camera for the sole purpose of the "Scan" simulation feature.

  • No images or videos are recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • The camera feed is used only locally on your device to create an interactive experience for the user.

3. Local Storage

Any data created within the App (such as custom items, prices, or cart history) is stored locally on your device.

  • We do not sync this data to any cloud servers.
  • Deleting the App will remove all locally stored data.

4. Hardware Connectivity (Printers)

The App may request permission to connect to local devices (e.g., via Bluetooth or Local Network) to communicate with Thermal Printers (ZPL/Zebra).

  • This connection is used strictly to print "pretend receipts."
  • No data from your network or device is collected during this process.
